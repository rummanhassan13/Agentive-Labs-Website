## The Challenge

Apex Logistics, a regional logistics provider, received over 1,500 WhatsApp messages daily from customers asking about package statuses, shipping rates, and delivery delays. Because messages were landing in a generic shared inbox, team members spent hours manually sorting and responding. The average response time exceeded 2 hours, violating SLAs and frustrating key corporate accounts.

## The Solution

We built an intelligent, context-aware triage assistant:
1. **Interactive Greeting & Triage:** Incoming WhatsApp messages are parsed in real-time. A fast classifier determines the user intent (Tracking, Scoping/Quote, Support).
2. **AI Semantic Search:** For common FAQs (e.g., "What are your delivery regions?"), the n8n flow runs a vector similarity search in Supabase using `pgvector` to fetch the exact policy, drafting an instantaneous, human-vetted reply.
3. **CRM Routing:** For complex service queries, a new HubSpot ticket is created immediately, populated with the full conversation transcript, and assigned to the matching regional operations manager.
4. **Instant Escalation:** If the user displays high frustration, the system bypasses AI and instantly alerts an on-duty human operator on Slack, pulling up a live chat interface.

## The ROI & Results

Within the first 60 days of launching the agent:
* **First Response:** Average first-response time dropped to under 10 seconds for FAQs, and under 8 minutes for human handoffs.
* **Support Deflection:** 40% of standard tracking and FAQ inquiries are completely handled by the assistant.
* **Revenue Preservation:** Saved 3 accounts that were on the verge of churn due to poor communication speeds.
