## The Challenge

Our client, a high-growth Shopify D2C brand operating at $4M ARR, was bogged down by a massive operational bottleneck. Every day, the finance team spent 5 to 6 hours manually cross-referencing Shopify orders, Stripe transaction receipts, and Xero invoices. Due to discrepancies in refunds, multi-currency conversions, and tax calculations, simple copy-paste errors led to inaccurate reporting and missed refund leakages. 

## The Solution

We designed and implemented a custom multi-stage data orchestration pipeline using **n8n** and **Supabase**:
1. **Extraction:** A web-hook listener polls real-time order events from Shopify and transaction settlements from Stripe.
2. **Matching Engine:** Transactions are consolidated in a secure Supabase ledger, where currency rates are normalized and matched against corresponding Xero invoices.
3. **Automated Ledgering:** Successful matches automatically reconcile in Xero with zero human intervention.
4. **Exception Handling:** Unmatched charges are routed to a secure Slack-alerted exceptions queue panel, where teams can review and manually force-pair with a single click.

## The ROI & Results

Within the first 30 days of deployment:
* **Time Saved:** Daily reconciliation dropped from 6 hours to under 15 minutes of overview checking.
* **Recovered Revenue:** Detected and flagged $14,000 in duplicate refunds and miscalculated currency transactions that would have gone unnoticed.
* **Accuracy:** Reached 100% bookkeeping audit readiness.
